Final Fantasy I (Pixel Remaster) — Chapter 2: Chaos Shrine / Temple of Fiends (Rescue)

Step-by-step

1. Enter the Chaos Shrine / Temple of Fiends.
2. From the entrance area, go to the accessible side rooms and open the available chests:
3. Open chest: Leather Cap (accessible now). :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
4. Open chest: Tent (accessible now). :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
5. Open chest: Potion (accessible now). :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
6. Do not worry about the right-side locked rooms/chests yet (they open later with a key). :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
7. Return to the main hall and go north to confront Garland.
8. After the fight, speak to Princess Sarah to receive the Lute.
9. Watch the bridge event/opening and exit to the world map.
Treasure / Extras checklist (Chapter 2)
Chaos Shrine / Temple of Fiends (present):
  • Chests obtained now: Leather Cap, Tent, Potion. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}
  • More chests appear on the in-game counter (Pixel Remaster), but some are not obtainable until later. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

Final Fantasy I (Pixel Remaster) — Chapter 2: Chaos Shrine / Temple of Fiends

Step-by-step

1. Enter the Chaos Shrine / Temple of Fiends.
2. From the entrance, go to all accessible side rooms.
3. Open the following chests:
  • Leather Cap
  • Tent
  • Potion
4. Ignore any locked doors or inaccessible areas.
5. Return to the main hall and go north.
6. Defeat Garland.
7. Speak to Princess Sarah.
8. Return to Castle Cornelia.
9. Speak to the King.
10. Watch the bridge event and exit to the world map.
Treasure / Extras checklist (Chapter 2)
• Chaos Shrine (present): Leather Cap, Tent, Potion
• Remaining chests in this location are obtained later.

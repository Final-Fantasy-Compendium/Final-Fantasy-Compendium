Final Fantasy I (Pixel Remaster) — Chapter 2: Chaos Shrine / Temple of Fiends

Step-by-step

1. Enter the Chaos Shrine / Temple of Fiends.
2. From the entrance, open all chests you can reach now:
  • Open the chest containing Leather Cap.
  • Open the chest containing Tent.
  • Open the chest containing Potion.
3. Do not worry about any doors/areas you cannot access yet. Leave them for later.
4. Return to the main hall and go north to the boss room.
5. Fight Garland.
6. After the fight, speak to Princess Sarah.
7. Return to Castle Cornelia.
8. Speak to the King to complete the objective.
9. After the bridge event, exit to the world map.
Treasure / Extras checklist (Chapter 2)
• Chaos Shrine (present): Leather Cap, Tent, Potion obtained.
• Any other chests/doors in this location are handled later when they become accessible.

Final Fantasy I (Pixel Remaster) — Chapter 2: Temple of Fiends

Your first dungeon: safe pacing, resource management, and boss prep.

Game: FFI (Pixel Remaster) Chapter: 2 Estimated time: 30–60 min Spoiler level: Low
Chapter goal: Clear the Temple of Fiends and win the boss fight without over-grinding.

On this page

Goals Prep Checklist Dungeon Route (Simple) Enemy Tips Boss Strategy Loot & Notes Next Chapter

Goals

  • Reach the boss with enough HP/items to recover from bad turns.
  • Learn “safe dungeon rhythm”: fight → top off → push forward → retreat if needed.
  • Beat the boss cleanly with focus fire and smart healing.

Prep Checklist

Recommended before entering

  • Rest at the Inn so you start full.
  • Bring 8–12 Potions total for the party.
  • Make sure at least 1–2 characters have upgraded weapons.
  • Save before you leave Cornelia.

If you’re struggling

  • Fight a few battles near Cornelia to stabilize.
  • Buy 1 more key armor piece for the character getting hit most.
  • Enter again and aim for fewer wasted turns (focus fire).
Rule of thumb: If you’re forced to heal every single battle, you’re either undergeared or spreading damage too thin.

Dungeon Route (Simple)

  • The Temple is a straightforward “rooms + halls” layout.
  • Explore calmly and don’t push deeper if you’re already low on Potions.
  • If you find a room that feels like it’s draining you, back out and reset.

Save/Reset rhythm

  • After a rough fight: heal up, then decide if you should retreat.
  • Retreating is efficient. Town is your “refuel station.”

Enemy Tips

  • Focus fire: take one enemy off the field quickly instead of spreading damage.
  • If you have multiple enemies hitting hard, prioritize the one that can finish a low-HP party member.
  • Don’t be afraid to use a Potion earlier than “you think you should” — early deaths waste more time than a Potion.

Boss Strategy

Boss fight checklist

  • Before entering: heal everyone to full.
  • Turn plan: attack with your best hitters and keep HP safe.
  • Healing: if someone drops low, heal immediately—don’t gamble.
  • Win condition: consistent damage + no panic turns.
Common wipe cause: trying to “save” Potions and then losing a character. Heal earlier; you’ll finish faster and safer.

Loot & Notes

  • No major “permanent missables” in this early section for Pixel Remaster.
  • Your real reward here is momentum: once this is done, progression speeds up.

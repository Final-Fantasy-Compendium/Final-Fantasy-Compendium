Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 2: Sector 7 Slums & Avalanche Base

Format: Step-by-step (main progression) + separate Optional/Missable checklist.

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/sector-7-slums.png.

Step-by-step

1. After exiting the train, follow the party to the Sector 7 Slums.
2. Proceed through the slums until you reach Seventh Heaven.
3. Enter Seventh Heaven to trigger the story scene.
4. After the scene, follow the group downstairs to the Avalanche hideout.
5. Speak to the party members until the next story trigger occurs.
6. When prompted, rest and continue the story.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 2)
These items and interactions can be missed if you advance the story.
Sector 7 Slums
• Potion — Speak to the boy near the slum entrance.
• Ether — Speak to the man standing near Seventh Heaven.
• All Materia — Talk to the woman inside Seventh Heaven (upper floor) after the initial scene.
Notes:
• Enemy Skill Materia is not obtainable yet.
• These NPC rewards cannot be collected later if skipped.

Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 24: Underwater Reactor & Submarine

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist.

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/underwater-reactor-submarine.png.

Step-by-step

1. Follow the story route to the Underwater Reactor.
2. Proceed through the reactor’s corridors and complete required encounters.
3. Finish the chapter’s main objective inside the reactor.
4. After the reactor, continue into the Submarine segment as directed.
5. Save once you have free movement again after acquiring the submarine.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 24)
This chapter unlocks major world-map mobility. Save immediately after you regain control.
Post-submarine checkpoint
• Save as soon as you can—this is a major progression milestone.
• Use your new mobility later for optional content, not during the first pass unless the route calls for it.
Notes:
• Optional underwater exploration is best handled on your dedicated optional chapters.

Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 3: Reactor 5 Bombing Mission

Format: Step-by-step (main progression) + separate Optional/Missable checklist.

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/reactor-5.png.

Step-by-step

1. After resting at Seventh Heaven, follow the party to the train.
2. Board the train and remain seated until you reach the Sector 5 platform.
3. Exit the train and proceed into Reactor 5.
4. Move through the corridors and catwalks toward the reactor interior.
5. Before entering the core area, check side rooms for pickups listed below.
6. Descend into the reactor core and select Set timer when prompted.
7. Escape the reactor along the same route.
8. Defeat the boss encounter during the escape.
9. Continue fleeing until the cutscene triggers.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 3)
These items are permanently missable if you leave the reactor without collecting them.
Reactor 5 pickups
• Potion — Chest in the early side corridor.
• Ether — Chest along the upper catwalks.
• Hi-Potion — Chest closer to the core area.
• Limit Break: Blade Beam — Automatically learned after defeating the boss.
Notes:
• Timer choice does not affect missables.
• Enemy Skill Materia is still not obtainable.
• The Limit Break is automatic and cannot be missed.

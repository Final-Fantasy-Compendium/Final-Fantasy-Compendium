Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 22: Icicle Inn & Snowfields

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist.

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/icicle-inn-snowfields.png.

Step-by-step

1. Arrive at Icicle Inn and explore the town briefly.
2. Handle the required story scenes in Icicle Inn.
3. Prepare for an extended travel segment through the Snowfields.
4. Proceed through the snow route until you reach the next major area.
5. Save at the first safe opportunity after completing the snow travel segment.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 22)
The Snowfields can be disorienting. Keep a simple “forward progress” mindset and save often.
Preparation
• Restock healing items before leaving Icicle Inn.
• If you’re low on resources, do not push deep into the snow segment without a save.
Notes:
• Any optional exploration is best done once you’re stable on the route.

Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 23: Gaea’s Cliff & Whirlwind Maze

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist.

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/gaea-cliff-whirlwind-maze.png.

Step-by-step

1. Enter Gaea’s Cliff and begin the climb.
2. Manage resources carefully—this segment can be endurance-heavy.
3. Continue upward through required routes until you reach the Whirlwind Maze.
4. Proceed through the Maze and complete all required story events.
5. Exit when the game advances you to the next major chapter.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 23)
This is one of the most demanding stretches. Keep your party healthy and your saves frequent.
Resource management
• Use inns/rest points when offered.
• Consider a short grind if you’re underleveled for the required fights.
Notes:
• This chapter is more about survival and progression than optional detours.

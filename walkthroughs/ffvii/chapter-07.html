Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 7: Escape from Midgar

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist.

Step-by-step

1. After the holding cell sequence, follow the party out of the Shinra Building.
2. Proceed through the building and board the elevator when prompted.
3. Continue following the escape route through the upper floors.
4. Exit the building and proceed onto the highway.
5. Complete the highway sequence to leave Midgar.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 7)
These items are permanently missable after leaving Midgar.
Shinra Building (final pass)
• Elixir — Chest in the upper floors during escape.
• Ether — Chest in the stairwell route.
Midgar exit
• No additional pickups after the highway sequence.
Notes:
• This is the final opportunity to collect Midgar-only items.
• Enemy Skill learning in Midgar must be completed before this chapter ends.

Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 13: Cargo Ship & Costa del Sol

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist.

Map Optional reference

Step-by-step

1. Proceed to the Cargo Ship as directed by the story.
2. Explore the ship’s accessible areas and talk to required NPCs to advance.
3. Handle the mandatory conflict/event on the ship when it triggers.
4. After arriving, enter Costa del Sol.
5. Use Costa del Sol as a reset point: heal, shop, and save before moving on.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 13)
This chapter is mostly story movement. Use Costa del Sol as your “prep stop” before the Corel/Gold Saucer stretch.
Cargo Ship (quick sweep)
• Check any accessible rooms/corridors before you trigger the next major sequence.
Costa del Sol (prep stop)
• Restock items and save.
• If you’re following a strict 100% route, keep a note of any purchases you want to delay until later.
Notes:
• No permanent one-time collectibles are required in this chapter for the 100% route.

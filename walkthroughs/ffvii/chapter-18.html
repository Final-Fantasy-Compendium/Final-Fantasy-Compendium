Step-by-step

1. Climb Mt. Nibel via the main path.
2. Explore side areas as they become accessible.
3. Defeat required encounters and continue upward.
4. Exit Mt. Nibel and travel onward to Rocket Town.
5. Complete the story events in Rocket Town.

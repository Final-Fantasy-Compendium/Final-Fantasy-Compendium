Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 5: Wall Market & Don Corneo

Format: Step-by-step (main progression) + separate Optional/Missable checklist.

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/wall-market.png.

Step-by-step

1. After the sewer sequence, follow the path to Wall Market.
2. Explore Wall Market to advance the story.
3. Enter buildings as prompted by story scenes.
4. When ready, proceed to the mansion to continue the main plot.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 5)
These items, materia, and outcomes are permanently missable after this chapter.
Wall Market items
• Ether — Purchase from a shop.
• Materia: Steal — Purchase from the weapon shop.
Dress event outcomes
• One unique accessory is awarded depending on choices made.
• Only one accessory can be obtained per playthrough.
• To collect all versions, multiple playthroughs are required.
Mansion interior
• Ether — Chest on the upper floor.
• Phoenix Down — Chest inside the mansion.
Notes:
• No Enemy Skill Materia is obtainable yet.
• Choice outcomes here affect later scenes but do not block story completion.

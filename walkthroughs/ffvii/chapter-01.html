Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 1: Reactor 1 Bombing Mission

Format: Step-by-step (main progression) + separate Optional/Missable checklist.

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/reactor-1.png (folder and capitalization must match).

Step-by-step

1. Move forward from the train platform and follow the group into Reactor 1.
2. Proceed through the corridors until you reach the first elevator.
3. Before taking the elevator down, check the side room(s) for pickups listed in the Optional/Missable checklist below.
4. Take the elevator down and continue toward the reactor core.
5. When prompted at the core, select Set timer (any time value is fine).
6. After planting the bomb, backtrack along the same route toward the entrance.
7. Defeat the boss encounter when it appears.
8. Continue escaping until you reach the train and board when prompted.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 1)
These are items you can miss if you leave the reactor without picking them up.
Pickups inside Reactor 1
• Potion (chest near the entrance area)
• Potion (field pickup on the upper walkways)
• Phoenix Down (chest near the elevator / approach to the core)
• Restore Materia (field pickup at/near the core before setting the bomb)
Notes: The boss reward is automatic and does not require a checklist step.

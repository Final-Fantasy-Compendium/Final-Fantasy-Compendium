Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 14: Corel Region & Gold Saucer

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist.

Map Optional reference

Step-by-step

1. Leave Costa del Sol and travel into the Corel Region along the main route.
2. Reach North Corel and continue to the station that leads to the Gold Saucer.
3. Enter the Gold Saucer for the first time and follow the required story route.
4. Move through the mandatory introduction/sequence and continue when prompted.
5. When the chapter ends, you should be set up for the Corel Prison segment next.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 14)
Gold Saucer introduces lots of optional content. Most of it can be revisited later, so keep this chapter focused on clean progression.
Corel Region
• Stay on the main route; optional wandering can be done later if you want to grind.
Gold Saucer (first visit)
• Treat this as an “unlock” chapter: advance story first, then optional minigames later when the route calls for it.
Notes:
• Major Gold Saucer optional rewards are better handled on the dedicated revisit chapter in your outline.

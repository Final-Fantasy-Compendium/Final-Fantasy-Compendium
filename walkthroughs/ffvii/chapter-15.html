Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 15: Corel Prison & Desert Escape

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist.

Map Optional reference

Step-by-step

1. Begin in Corel Prison and follow the story scenes.
2. Complete the required prison events to progress.
3. Participate in the mandatory Chocobo Race sequence.
4. Win the race to earn your freedom.
5. Exit the prison area and return toward the Gold Saucer.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 15)
This chapter is mostly linear and story-driven. Winning the race is required to advance.
Chocobo Race
• Save beforehand if possible.
• Winning cleanly keeps the route simple and consistent.
Notes:
• No permanent missables are tied to the prison itself.

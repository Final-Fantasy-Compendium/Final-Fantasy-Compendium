Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 11: Mythril Mine & Fort Condor (1st Visit)

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist.

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/mythril-mine-fort-condor.png.

Step-by-step

1. After crossing the marsh, enter the cave on the far side and proceed into the Mythril Mine.
2. Follow the main path through the mine, clearing encounters as needed.
3. Exit the mine to reach the outdoor area leading toward Junon.
4. Optional: detour to Fort Condor for your first visit and a quick introduction to the area.
5. When finished, return to the main route and continue toward Junon.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 11)
This chapter is a short transition segment. Fort Condor is optional on the first pass, but visiting now keeps your 100% route organized.
Fort Condor (recommended)
• Visit at least once so it’s “on your radar” for later story moments.
• If you choose to participate in any fort activities, treat rewards as one-time for that visit.
Mythril Mine
• Stay on the main route and fully explore side pockets you can reach without backtracking.
Notes:
• Enemy Skill Materia has not been obtained yet.
• Nothing here is permanently missable on this visit, but this is a clean “checkpoint” before Junon.

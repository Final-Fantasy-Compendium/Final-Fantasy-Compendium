Final Fantasy VII (Original) — Chapter 8: Kalm & Nibelheim Flashback

Step-by-step main progression + optional/missable checklist. (No strategy, no spoilers beyond required scenes.)

Map Optional reference
If this image is missing, upload it to assets/ffvii/maps/kalm.png.

Step-by-step

1. After leaving Midgar, follow the world map path to Kalm.
2. Enter Kalm and go to the inn (the large building) to advance the story.
3. Continue the story scenes until the Nibelheim flashback begins.
4. During the flashback, follow the on-screen prompts and proceed through the required locations until the flashback ends.
5. After returning to the present in Kalm, exit town to continue your journey.
Optional / Missable checklist (Chapter 8)
This chapter has very few permanent missables. Use this checklist mainly to avoid confusion.
Kalm (present day)
• Optional: Check shops if you want to update supplies before leaving. (Not missable.)
• Optional: Talk to townspeople for one-time dialogue. (Not required for 100% items.)
Nibelheim flashback
• Items picked up during the flashback do not carry over to the present day inventory.
• No Enemy Skill Materia learning is required here.
